Hi there! I’m Antonia, a passionate developer based in Constanţa, Romania.

With a keen eye for detail and a love for clean, accessible design, I specialize in creating intuitive, pixel-perfect user interfaces that prioritize both aesthetics and performance. My journey in tech has led me through various roles, from freelancing and collaborating with startups to contributing to larger digital projects, each experience enriching my skill set and broadening my perspective.

urrently, I focus on building seamless web experiences, ensuring that every line of code serves a purpose and every design choice enhances usability. I believe in the power of thoughtful engineering to create products that not only function well but also delight users.

Outside of coding, I’m an avid reader, a lover of outdoor adventures, and a fan of exploring new technologies and design trends. Whether it's hiking through the Carpathian Mountains, diving into a good book, or experimenting with the latest web frameworks, I’m always eager to learn and grow.
